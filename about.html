Who we are!

Hard Rock Boxes was established 16 years ago in 2005, serving demand via eco-friendly products. It is one stop shop for all your need in packaging and manufacturong of corrugated boxes. We are committed to deliver the premium cases for all premium goods because we understand it has to be good both outside and inside. Today our solutions are found in segments as building, retail,food and beverages, manufacturing, publishing,pharmaceutical,cosmetics,confectionary, hygiene and textiles.

Tomorrow, we believe there is the potential to produce anything that's made with fossil-based materials from a tree. We have our manufacturing units established in Hazaribagh, Jharkhand and in Kosi Kalan,Uttar Pradesh. To execute with classified finishing packaging, we have a separate wing dedicated for Research and Development which primarly runs on the objective of coming up with new material solution everytime. Overall, we aim to be a leader in our field, preferred partner, and a respected member in the societies where we work, bringing value to our customers, employees, investors, suppliers, and other stakeholders.
